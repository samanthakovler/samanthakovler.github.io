Caesar Salad Recipe

Description

This Caesar salad is a fresh and flavorfu dish that brings everyone together around the table. Itâ€™s easy to love, always satisfying, and perfect for sharing at family dinners or gatherings. Light yet comforting, it has that timeless taste that never gets old.

Ingredients for Dressing

  • 3 anchovy fillets
  • 2 cloves garloic, finely chopped
  • 1/2 lemon, juiced
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 dash Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Salt and ground black pepper

Ingredients for Salad

  • 1/2 head romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons croutons

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. To make the Caesar dressing: Mash anchovy fillets and garlic in a large salad bowl
  3. Add lemon juice, Dijon mustard, egg yolk, and Worcestershire sauce; whisk until smooth and creamy
  4. Gradually stream in olive oil while whisking constantly. Season to taste with salt and black pepper
  5. To serve: Gently mix romaine lettuce and Parmesan cheese into dressing until thoroughly coated
  6. Top Caesar salad with croutons and enjoy!
